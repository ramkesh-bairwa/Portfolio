Skyline Residences

Luxury Residential Complex | Jaipur, Rajasthan

Project Overview

Skyline Residences is a premium residential complex designed for modern urban living. The project includes 50 luxury apartments with sustainable design features, smart home technology, and community spaces that promote social interaction while ensuring privacy.

Key Features

  • Sustainable design with rainwater harvesting and solar panels
  • Smart home automation system integration
  • Landscaped gardens and recreational areas
  • Energy-efficient building materials and insulation
  • Customizable interior layouts for residents
  • Security and surveillance systems

My Role

As the lead designer, I was responsible for the overall design concept, space planning, material selection, and coordinating with architects and engineers to ensure design integrity throughout the construction process.

Urban Loft Transformation

Residential Interior Design | Malviya Nagar, Jaipur

Project Overview

This project involved transforming a traditional 3BHK apartment into a modern urban loft with open-plan living, smart storage solutions, and a minimalist aesthetic. The design maximizes natural light and creates a sense of spaciousness while maintaining functionality for a family of four.

Design Highlights

  • Open-plan living area with kitchen integration
  • Custom built-in storage solutions
  • Minimalist color palette with warm wood accents
  • Multifunctional furniture for space optimization
  • Smart lighting system with different mood settings
  • Balcony converted to indoor garden space

Client Feedback

"Poonam transformed our cramped apartment into a spacious, functional home that reflects our personality. Her attention to detail and creative storage solutions have made our daily lives so much easier." - The Sharma Family
